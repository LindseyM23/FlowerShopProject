Flower Arrangements

Elegant Rose Bouquet

This stunning bouquet features a carefully curated selection of premium long-stemmed roses in an array of colors, including classic green, soft pink, and purple.

Benefits: Roses are not only visually captivating but also have the potential to uplift moods and enhance the ambiance of any room.

Smell: The fragrance of roses is sweet and intoxicating, with each color variety offering subtle nuances in scent. The red and pink roses typically have a stronger, more traditional rose aroma, while white roses tend to be more delicate and less overpowering.

Longevity: With proper care, such as regular trimming and fresh water, these roses can remain vibrant and fragrant for up to 7-10 days.

Sunny Sunflower Arrangement

This cheerful arrangement is composed of bright, sun-kissed sunflowers paired with a mix of seasonal greenery and white roses.

Benefits: Their bold, sunny appearance can lift spirits and add a warm, welcoming vibe to any room.

Smell: Sunflowers have a subtle, earthy scent that is pleasant but not overpowering, making them perfect for spaces where a strong floral fragrance might be too much.

Longevity: Sunflowers are quite durable, often lasting between 10-14 days with proper care, which includes changing the water frequently and keeping them in a cool environment.

Fragrant Lily Delight

This elegant arrangement showcases the exquisite beauty of oriental lilies, known for their large blooms and striking appearance. Available in shades of white and purple.

Benefits: Lilies symbolize purity, commitment, and rebirth. They are often used in weddings and special celebrations due to their elegant and sophisticated look. Additionally, their large blooms can serve as a focal point in any room.

Smell: Oriental lilies are famous for their powerful, sweet fragrance that can fill an entire room. This makes them an excellent choice for creating a fragrant, inviting atmosphere.

Longevity: With proper care, such as trimming the stems and changing the water regularly, oriental lilies can last from 10 to 14 days. Removing the pollen from the stamens can also extend their lifespan and prevent staining.

Delicate Orchid Ensemble

This exquisite arrangement features a variety of orchid blooms, known for their exotic appearance and delicate beauty. Available in colors ranging from red to white.

Benefits: Orchids are often associated with luxury, strength, and beauty. They can add a touch of elegance to any setting and are perfect for special occasions or as a refined gift.

Smell: Many orchid varieties have a light, pleasant fragrance. While some orchids may have no scent, others can surprise with a sweet or citrusy aroma, adding a subtle fragrance to your space.

Longevity: Orchids are known for their exceptional longevity, often lasting between 2-3 weeks with proper care. They require minimal maintenance, needing only occasional misting and indirect sunlight to thrive.
